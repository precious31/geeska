About Us

GEESKA| The Horn Centre of Ideas is an independent, non-partisan and non-profit organization. GEESKA is a centre of research and policy advocacy based organization. It was founded in 2019 by diverse professional backgrounds dedicated to the promotion of peace, order, good governance and human development in Somalia. The centre aims to generate new knowledge, work co-construct knowledge, partnership and dynamic environment, and institutional capacity to fit for purpose.

The centre provides the following:

  • Research
  • Education and training
  • Advocacy support to enhance peace, human development and good governance initiatives at all levels in the Horn of Africa and in particular Somalia
  • Policy recommendations for decision and policy makers

Geeska is a private, not-for- profit research and policy analysis think tank whose primary mission is to provide quality research and development services about Somalis in order to contribute to the attainment of the development priorities.

Key Strategic Focus Areas

The Centre has engaged in activities that helped initiate dialogue among the Somali people in The Netherlands. The Centre is committed to work with different partners to promote peace and human development through grassroots initiatives. The Centre provides training and capacity building programmes on specific topics for a wide variety of groups both local and regional organizations. The Centre conducts high quality research on matters related to peace, social justice and good governance. In close collaboration with higher education institutions in The Netherlands, the Centre hosts conferences, round table discussion and debates and symposia in support of different subjects in the broader area of the Centre’s thematic interest.
