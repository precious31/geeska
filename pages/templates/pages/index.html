About Us

Geeska is a center of research that generates new knowledge that aims to be relevant, original and excellent. Our work co-constructs knowledge, alliance, dynamic policy environment and institutional capacity to fit for purpose. We provide the following:

  • Evidence-based policy recommandetion for policy making
  • We advocate for research development through partnerships
  • Investing in research from grassroots

Geeska is a private, not-for- profit research and policy analysis think tank whose primary mission is to provide quality research and development services about Somalis in order to contribute to the attainment of the development priorities.
